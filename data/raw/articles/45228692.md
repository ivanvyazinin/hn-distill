# Matt Mullenweg

## Unlucky in Cards

](https://ma.tt/ "Matt Mullenweg")

Just got word that the [court dismissed several of WP Engine and Silver Lake’s most serious claims](https://www.courtlistener.com/docket/69221176/169/wpengine-inc-v-automattic-inc/) — antitrust, monopolization, and extortion have been knocked out! These were by far the most significant and far-reaching allegations in the case and with today’s decision the case is narrowed significantly. This is a win not just for us but for all open source maintainers and contributors. Huge thanks to the folks at [Gibson](https://www.gibsondunn.com/) and [Automattic](https://automattic.com/) who have been working on this.

With respect to any remaining claims, we’re confident the facts will demonstrate that our actions were lawful and in the best interests of the WordPress community.

This ruling is a significant milestone, but our focus remains the same: building a free, open, and thriving WordPress ecosystem and supporting the millions of people who rely on it every day.
