## Apple Intelligence

Get more done, from even more places.

Now integrated into even more apps and experiences, Apple Intelligence helps you get things done effortlessly and communicate across languages.[3](#footnote-3)

*   ### Live Translation.
    
    Automatically translate texts in Messages,[4](#footnote-4) display live translated captions in FaceTime, and get spoken translations for calls in the Phone app.[5](#footnote-5)
    
     ![Live Translation in Messages with the user’s text message “Adriana and I will invite some friends to dinner on Sunday night. Do you want to come?” automatically translated into Spanish](/v/os/c/images/shared/apple_intelligence/live_translation__x2fac526k0im_large.jpg)

*   ### Accelerate your workflows.
    
    Intelligent actions in Shortcuts can summarize text, create images, or tap directly into Apple Intelligence models to provide responses that feed into your shortcut.
    
     ![Compare notes in Text and Transcription then summarize, with the words 'Text' and 'Transcription' highlighted in blue as they appear as they would in the Shortcuts app](/v/os/c/images/shared/apple_intelligence/icon_ask_model__d1tqjy3fi3ma_large.jpg)

*   ### More ways to express yourself with images.
    
    Mix emoji and descriptions to make something brand-new. In Image Playground, discover additional ChatGPT styles. And have even more control when making images inspired by family and friends using Genmoji and Image Playground.[6](#footnote-6)
    
     ![Image Playground screen with image of a photorealistic storks carrying a bundle in its beak, showing options for custom image styles from ChatGPT](/v/os/c/images/shared/apple_intelligence/genmoji__b2htoaj2to76_large.jpg)

## Continuity

Together they’re better than ever.

Continuity helps you work seamlessly across Apple devices. And with the Phone app and Live Activities coming to Mac, it’s even easier to stay on top of things happening in real time.

*   ### Live Activities on Mac.
    
    The menu bar now features the Live Activities from your iPhone. And when you click one, the app opens in iPhone Mirroring so you can take action.[2](#footnote-2)
    
     ![Top of Mac device screen showing the menu bar with an expanded Live Activity for Uber Eats](/v/os/c/images/macos/continuity_communication/live_activities__c1eig85sdfiq_large.jpg)
    
     ![Top of Mac device screen showing the menu bar with an expanded Live Activity for Uber Eats](/v/os/c/images/macos/continuity_communication/live_activities__c1eig85sdfiq_large.jpg)
    
*   ### New Phone app for Mac.
    
    Make and take calls with a click. Conveniently access your synced content like Recents, Contacts, and Voicemail — and enjoy the familiar features from iPhone.[1](#footnote-1)
    
     ![FaceTime Contact Poster, Genmoji in center, person’s name top left, icons for reply, more options, hang up, and pick up on the bottom](/v/os/c/images/macos/continuity_communication/mac_phone__e2u9ab76th8i_large.jpg)
    
     ![FaceTime Contact Poster, Genmoji in center, person’s name top left, icons for reply, more options, hang up, and pick up on the bottom](/v/os/c/images/macos/continuity_communication/mac_phone__e2u9ab76th8i_large.jpg)
    
*   ### Manage unwanted calls.
    
    For unknown numbers, Call Screening finds out who’s calling and why. Once the caller shares their name and the reason for their call, your phone rings and you can decide if you want to pick up.[7](#footnote-7)
    
     ![Call Screening showing Screening New Caller notification](/v/os/c/images/macos/continuity_communication/calls__f2ec2qqhtpei_large.jpg)
    
     ![Call Screening showing Screening New Caller notification](/v/os/c/images/macos/continuity_communication/calls__f2ec2qqhtpei_large.jpg)
    
*   ### Stay productive while on hold.
    
    Hold Assist keeps your spot in line while you wait for a live agent and notifies you when they’re ready.[8](#footnote-8)
    
     ![Right corner of Mac screen with Hold Assist Live Activity indicating user is on hold with a customer service representative](/v/os/c/images/shared/continuity_communication/hold_assist__de7h86bgr2gm_large.jpg)
    
     ![Right corner of Mac screen with Hold Assist Live Activity indicating user is on hold with a customer service representative](/v/os/c/images/shared/continuity_communication/hold_assist__de7h86bgr2gm_large.jpg)

## macOS Tahoe features And so much more.

*   [Explore All New Features (PDF)](/os/pdf/All_New_Features_macOS_Tahoe_Sept_2025.pdf)

 ![](/v/os/c/images/shared/more/icon_accessibility__ey38qgbdpiuu_large.png)

### Accessibility.

Magnifier lets you zoom in on your surroundings using a connected camera. Accessibility Reader provides a systemwide, customized reading and listening experience. Braille Access creates an all-new interface for braille displays.[9](#footnote-9) And Vehicle Motion Cues help reduce motion sickness in moving vehicles.

 ![](/v/os/c/images/shared/more/icon_family_sharing__etze23t93rwy_large.png)

 ![](/v/os/c/images/shared/more/icon_family_sharing__etze23t93rwy_large.png)

### Family.

Parents can take advantage of a wide set of parental controls designed to keep children safe. These include new enhancements across Communication Limits, Communication Safety, and the App Store.

 ![](/v/os/c/images/shared/more/icon_journal__c2vx8vx6e0eq_large.png)

 ![](/v/os/c/images/shared/more/icon_journal__c2vx8vx6e0eq_large.png)

### Journal.

Now on Mac for the most comfortable writing experience, Journal makes it easy to capture and write about everyday moments and special events using photos, videos, audio recordings, places, and more.

 ![](/v/os/c/images/shared/more/icon_photos__c1mo1v0dm1ci_large.png)

 ![](/v/os/c/images/shared/more/icon_photos__c1mo1v0dm1ci_large.png)

### Photos.

An updated design lets you quickly access filtering and sorting options and customize the size of Collections tiles so you can view your library just how you like. And with Pinned Collections, you can keep your most-visited ones right at your fingertips.

 ![](/v/os/c/images/macos/more/icon_facetime__feotyd7la8i2_large.png)

 ![](/v/os/c/images/macos/more/icon_facetime__feotyd7la8i2_large.png)

### FaceTime.

Celebrate the people who matter most with a new tiled design that features beautiful and personalized Contact Posters.

 ![](/v/os/c/images/shared/more/icon_reminders__cts245t4xcsy_large.png)

 ![](/v/os/c/images/shared/more/icon_reminders__cts245t4xcsy_large.png)

### Reminders.

With Apple Intelligence, Reminders can suggest tasks, grocery items, and follow-ups based on emails or other text on your device. It can also automatically categorize related reminders into sections within a list.

 ![](/v/os/c/images/shared/more/icon_gaming__faqjmo0zhvu6_large.png)

 ![](/v/os/c/images/shared/more/icon_gaming__faqjmo0zhvu6_large.png)

### Games.

The new Games app brings together all the games you have on your Mac. In the Game Overlay, you can adjust system settings, chat with friends, or invite them to play — all without leaving the game. And for developers, Metal 4 brings even more advanced graphics and rendering technologies, like MetalFX Frame Interpolation and Denoising.

 ![](/v/os/c/images/macos/more/icon_messages__c75oi8dpw4gi_large.png)

 ![](/v/os/c/images/macos/more/icon_messages__c75oi8dpw4gi_large.png)

### Messages.

Create polls and personalize conversations with backgrounds. Redesigned conversation details feature designated sections for contact info, photos, links, location, and more. Typing indicators in groups let you know exactly who is about to chime in. Screening tools detect spam and give you control. And the Add Contact button now appears next to an unknown number in a group.

 ![](/v/os/c/images/macos/more/icon_passwords__c7rqhn941eoi_large.png)

 ![](/v/os/c/images/macos/more/icon_passwords__c7rqhn941eoi_large.png)

### Passwords.

Easily refer to changes you’ve made to your accounts. Find previous versions of passwords, along with when they were changed.

 ![](/v/os/c/images/shared/more/icon_notes__b48ojk6xh7py_large.png)

 ![](/v/os/c/images/shared/more/icon_notes__b48ojk6xh7py_large.png)

### Notes.

Capture conversations in the Phone app as audio recordings with transcriptions.[10](#footnote-10) You can also export a note into a Markdown file.
