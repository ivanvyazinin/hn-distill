BookmarkSave

Cryptocurrency prices tumbled after US President Donald Trump said he would impose an additional 100% [tariff on China and export controls on software](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-10-10/trump-says-he-sees-no-reason-to-meet-xi-threatens-new-tariffs). Traders saw more than $7 billion in positions liquidated in less than an hour, according to data from Coinglass.

Bitcoin initially fell more than 12% after Trump made the announcement via a post on his Truth Social platform. The largest token, which had earlier this week [reached an all-time high](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-10-05/bitcoin-rises-to-a-record-as-debasement-trade-spurs-risk-rally) of more than $125,000, was trading around $113,000 as of Friday evening in New York.
