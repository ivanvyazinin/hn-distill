# Wan-Animate: Unified Character Animation and Replacement with Holistic Replication

With Wan-Animate, animate any static character by transferring movements, expressions, and gestures from a reference video. Replace characters seamlessly while preserving lighting and color tones for truly lifelike videos.

Wan-Animate can animate any character based on a performer's video, precisely replicating the performer's facial expressions and movements to generate highly realistic character videos.
