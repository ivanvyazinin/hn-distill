**Describe the bug**  
When Droid-ify tried to update Aves Libre to 1.13.9, I’ve got a popup from Google Play scanner telling me something like "this software could be dangerous". I was able to give my approval and install this version anyway.

**To Reproduce**  
Steps to reproduce the behavior:

1.  Enable security scanner in Google Play
2.  Attempt to install Aves Libre 1.13.9
3.  Presumably see the warning

**Expected behavior**  
Installation without warnings
