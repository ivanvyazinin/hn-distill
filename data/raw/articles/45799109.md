#### [Lattice Basis Reduction](https://cryptography101.ca/lattice-basis-reduction/)

An introduction to the LLL lattice basis reduction algorithm, one of the most powerful and versatile tools in cryptanalysis.

November 2025

[![](https://cryptography101.ca/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Screenshot-2025-07-26-at-4.25.43-AM.png)](https://cryptography101.ca/lattice-basis-reduction/)

* * *

#### [Kyber and Dilithium](/kyber-dilithium)

An introduction to the NIST-standardized quantum-safe key encapsulation and signature schemes based on lattices.

August 2024

[![](https://cryptography101.ca/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/kyberdilithium.png)](https://cryptography101.ca/kyber-dilithium/)

* * *

#### [Hash-Based Signature Schemes](https://cryptography101.ca/hash-based-signature-schemes/)

An introduction to quantum-safe signature schemes based on hash functions, including LMS and SPHINCS+.

June 2025

[![](https://cryptography101.ca/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/Hash-sigs.png)](https://cryptography101.ca/hash-based-signature-schemes/)

* * *

#### [The Mathematics of Lattice-Based Cryptography](https://cryptography101.ca/lattice-based-cryptography/)

An introduction to the core mathematics behind lattice-based cryptographic systems such as Kyber and Dilithium.

January 2025

[![](https://cryptography101.ca/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/lattices-logo-4.png)](https://cryptography101.ca/lattice-based-cryptography/)

* * *

#### [Applied Cryptography 101: Building Blocks](https://cryptography101.ca/crypto101-building-blocks)

This course covers the fundamental cryptographic primitives.

November 2024

[![](https://cryptography101.ca/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/building-blocks-1.png)](https://cryptography101.ca/crypto101-building-blocks/)

#### [Applied Cryptography 101: Real-World Deployments](https://cryptography101.ca/crypto101-deployments/)

I’ll present several case studies of how the fundamental cryptographic primitives are used to secure large-scale applications.

April 2025
