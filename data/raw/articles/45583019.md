 **The World's only  
Salt and Pepper Shaker Museum  
is in Gatlinburg, TN,  
  Gateway to the  
Great Smoky Mountain  
National Park**

When you find yourself in this part of the world stop by and see over 20,000 sets of Salt and Pepper Shakers from around the world, and a huge Pepper Mill collection!

You can visit the [Virtual Museum](/MuseumPictures) where you will see a  
sneak preview of the only\* museum of its kind.

Go to the [Location page](/Location) to get directions

Visit us on our [Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/pages/The-Salt-and-Pepper-Shaker-Museum/292317091118)  
 on [TripAdvisor](https://goo.gl/JCqs5)  
and on [Google](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Salt+%26+Pepper+Shaker+Museum/@35.7162371,-83.5046688,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m7!3m6!1s0x0:0x268f1a50e261bf53!8m2!3d35.7162371!4d-83.5024801!9m1!1b1)
