# The Other Linux Logo

Hi Linux lovers, feel free to share, use and edit this Linux logo we have designed for you.

Edit 2020

After seeing to your reactions on [Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/linux/comments/57sm0s/the_other_linux_logo/) and [omg! ubuntu!](https://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2016/10/alternative-tux-logo), we added some new options for wider usecases.
