# Arm Announces Lumex Platform With C1 CPUs Boasting SME2, Mali G1-Ultra GPU

Written by [Michael Larabel](https://www.michaellarabel.com/) in [Arm](/linux/Arm) on 9 September 2025 at 10:00 PM EDT. [8 Comments](/forums/node/1575865)

![ARM](/assets/categories/arm.webp)

Arm this evening lifted the lid on Lumex, their new compute subsystem platform that is purpose-built around AI for next-gen PCs and smartphones.  

[![Arm Lumex](//www.phoronix.net/image.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_1_med)](//www.phoronix.com/image-viewer.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_1_lrg)

  
The Arm Lumex platform combines their latest AArch64 CPUs complete with Scalable Matrix Extensions 2 (SME2), the latest GPU IP, and other system IP along with an optimized software stack for a very comprehensive AI-focused platform.  

[![Arm Lumex CSS platform](//www.phoronix.net/image.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_2_med)](//www.phoronix.com/image-viewer.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_2_lrg)

  
The Arm Lumexp latform features SME2-enabled Armv9.3 CPUs such as the C1 Ultra and C1 Pro, a new C1 Premium lower-tier offering, the new Mali G1-Ultra GPU, the DynamIQ Shared Unit DSU, and deep software integration.  

[![Arm Lumex features](//www.phoronix.net/image.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_3_med)](//www.phoronix.com/image-viewer.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_3_lrg)

  

[![Arm C1 CPU evolution](//www.phoronix.net/image.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_4_med)](//www.phoronix.com/image-viewer.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_4_lrg)

  
The Arm C1 Ultra CPU aims for +25% single-threaded performance and double-digit IPC gains YoY while below that are the C1 Premium, C1 Pro, and C1 Nano.  

[![Arm C1 CPU capabilities](//www.phoronix.net/image.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_5_med)](//www.phoronix.com/image-viewer.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_5_lrg)

  
A big focus on the media presentation in advance was the guarantee that Arm Lumex will have Scalable Matrix Extensions 2 support widespread. About time seeing more SME hardware in the while and in turn will also help with increased software adoption. Just as AVX-512 and even AMX have proven very useful in the x86\_64 ecosystem for AI workloads.  

[![Arm Lumex with SME2](//www.phoronix.net/image.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_6_med)](//www.phoronix.com/image-viewer.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_6_lrg)

  

[![Arm Lumex numbers](//www.phoronix.net/image.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_7_med)](//www.phoronix.com/image-viewer.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_7_lrg)

  

[![Arm Lumex overview](//www.phoronix.net/image.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_8_med)](//www.phoronix.com/image-viewer.php?id=2025&image=arm_lumex_8_lrg)

  
Those are the main Arm Lumex highlights for now. It will be much more interesting once having new Arm hardware in the lab for Linux testing and benchmarking.
