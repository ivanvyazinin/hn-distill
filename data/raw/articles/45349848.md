Today, we're starting to roll out the conversational editing feature [first introduced on Pixel 10](https://blog.google/products/photos/ai-photo-editing-google-photos/) to all [eligible](https://support.google.com/photos/answer/15318661?hl=en&co=GENIE.Platform%3DAndroid) Android users in the U.S. Forget switching between tools and adjusting sliders. You can simply describe the edits you want using your voice or text and watch Google Photos bring your vision to life. And now even more people can try it.

Just tap “Help me edit” in the editor, describe what you want changed and voila, Photos takes care of it for you — with a little help from advanced Gemini capabilities. Not sure where to start? Use one of the provided suggestions or simply say “make it better.” Or get creative with out-of-this-world edits like transporting an alpaca from a petting zoo to Waikiki (tropical beverage included).

With [simple gestures and one-tap editing suggestions](https://blog.google/products/photos/google-photos-10-years-tips-tricks/), [AI-powered creative tools](https://blog.google/products/photos/photo-to-video-remix-create-tab/) and now conversational AI editing, it’s never been easier to bring your creativity to life.
