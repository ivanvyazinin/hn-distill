### About us

EgoScience is an innovation-driven company that turns imaginative ideas into real products. Our team brings proven consumer-robot experience, from robot vacuums to drones, and strong in-house technical capabilities: we design and produce our own chips and sensors to keep core technologies in our control. We are also backed by industrial design talent to handle product appearance and content design, while an experienced engineering team leads EVT, development, and testing. With integrated hardware-software expertise and cross-disciplinary teams, EgoScience builds imaginative, practical products that make your ideas come to life.
