## Simplex just works.

### Production-ready

Eval harnesses to stress-test your workflows at scale and emulate production conditions.

### Authentication Handling

Authentication SDK functions to handle 2FA, login data, and more on your customers' sites. See more [here](https://www.simplex.sh/docs/sdk-reference/constructor#authentication).

### Scalable Headless Browsers

Headless browsers that can scale to 100s of concurrent sessions in seconds.

### Stealth Mode

Automatic CAPTCHA solving, proxies, and anti-bot protections.

### Controllable Workflows

Our web agents are constrained to only take the actions you tell it to.

### Optimized Workflows

Automatically cache your workflows for fast and reliable execution in production.

### Robust SDKs

Our SDKs are designed to be robust and easy to use. Available in both Python and TypeScript.

### Detailed Logging and Replays

View a livestream and live logs of sessions as they happen. Share session replays and detailed agent logs with your team and customers.
