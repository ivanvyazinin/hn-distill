Nobuo YAMAMOTO

    When we search for the internet about an oval curve, several sites of [Egg Curves and Ovals](http://www.mathematische-basteleien.de/eggcurves.htm), [Oval](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oval) and [Cassini oval](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cassini_oval) are found.    But there seem to be little equations of a curve near a real egg shape.    So, an equation of egg shaped curve which resembles closely to the shape of a chicken egg is pursued here apart from a mathematical definition of "oval curve".  
    This starts from the short article that Yamamoto has published in [Library Newsletter No. 63 (2006)](https://www.ibaraki-ct.ac.jp/lib/internal/tayori/2006/08.pdf) of Ibaraki National College of Technology.     Succeedingly, "Equation of Egg Shaped Curve of the Actual Egg is Found" is released in this page on Apr. 27, 2007,   ["Equation of Egg Shaped Curve II (Egg Shaped Curve I proposed by Mr. Itou"](index_egg_by_Itou_E.html) is released on Mar. 29, 2008,   ["Equation of Egg Shaped Curve III (Egg Shaped Curve I proposed by Mr. Asai)"](index_egg_by_SuudokuJuku_E.html) is released on Aug. 02, 2009,   ["Equation of Egg Shaped Curve IV (Egg Shaped Curve II proposed by Mr. Asai)"](index_egg_by_SuudokuJuku2_E.html) is released on Sep. 18, 2009,   ["Equation of Egg Shaped Curve V"](index_egg5_E.html) is released on Nov. 08, 2009,   ["Equation of Egg Shaped Curve VI"](index_egg6_E.html) is released on Nov. 26, 2009,   and ["Egg Shaped Curve VII (Egg Shaped Curve III proposed by Mr. Asai)"](index_egg_by_SuudokuJuku3_E.html) is released on May. 29, 2011.     For a while after these, there was no publication of an oval curve close to the shape of a real chicken egg.     However, as of March 2020, ["Equation to fit an egg"](https://www.johndcook.com/blog/2018/04/18/equation-to-fit-an-egg/), ["HÜGELSCHÄFFER EGG"](https://www.mathcurve.com/courbes2d.gb/oeuf/oeuf.shtml). ["Egg Curve"](https://www.desmos.com/calculator/agdyomzxxf), ["Egg-curve"](https://www.geogebra.org/m/wbsJE9TQ), ["Let's display an egg curve"](https://blog.studiofruitjam.com/2017/04/20/egg-curve/) and ["The Python Egg Curve"](https://pythonturtle.academy/tutorial-drawing-egg-shape-with-python-turtle/) have been issued.
