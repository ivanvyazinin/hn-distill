## Feature Highlights

### Live Ping Monitoring

![Live Ping Monitor](images/white/LivePing.png)

Track critical hosts in real time. See latency, uptime, and response trends instantly — know what’s healthy and what’s not.

### Speed Testing

![Speed Test](images/white/FullSpeedTest.png)

Measure download, upload, and latency with Cloudflare’s edge-powered speed tests, then share results instantly with clients or teammates.

### Interface Details

![Interface Details](images/white/FullInterfaceDetails.png)

Everything you need to know about your connection — from IP and DHCP to Wi-Fi strength and ISP details — presented clearly, updated in real time.

### Wireless Network Analysis

![Wireless Network Overview](images/white/FullAPDetails.png)

See exactly how your Wi-Fi is performing — live signal strength, channel usage, and nearby networks at a glance.

### Subnet Calculator

![Subnet Calculator](images/white/SubnetCalculator.png)

Plan and calculate networks in seconds — CIDR, masks, and ranges made simple and beautifully visual.

### MAC Address Lookup

![Wireless Network Details](images/white/MACAddressLookup.png)

Instantly look up vendors with a built-in offline OUI database that also flags randomized or locally administered MAC addresses — so you always know what’s real.

### Wi-Fi Sniffing/Monitor Mode

![Wi-Fi Monitor Mode](images/white/MonitorMode.png)

Turn your Mac into a Wi-Fi sniffer: choose a channel, hit Start, and we’ll handle monitor mode and open Wireshark for you. Change channels on the fly.

### Network Logging

![Wi-Fi Monitor Mode](images/white/NetworkLogsWithVLANData.png)

When PingStalker is running, it’s watching your network so you don’t have to. It listens for the chatter that matters — another host pinging you, a trunk port exposing extra VLANs, LLDP/CDP Neighbor information or a flood of DHCP or broadcast traffic. These are just a few of the events PingStalker automatically detects and logs, giving you eyes on everything happening beneath the surface.
