# ChatGPT Atlas

[

Download for macOS\*

](https://persistent.oaistatic.com/atlas/public/ChatGPT_Atlas.dmg)

Currently only available on macOS

Bring ChatGPT with you across the web for instant answers, smarter suggestions, and help with tasks—all with privacy settings you can control.

## Unlock the web with ChatGPT by your side

![Side chat running in browser](https://images.ctfassets.net/8su2tbn87fck/5xCqAIxzR1Vr4crwWhamKk/2b958199a37e4060a1a7c5ccf690ff23/Sidechat__7_.png?q=90&fm=webp)

![Demo body media](https://images.ctfassets.net/8su2tbn87fck/6EDJyQvQ2Mo4ZhKtShxvei/e4dcc7f039c89000f4309ca6035291e2/Sidechat-eyebrow.svg?q=90)

Open a ChatGPT sidebar in any window to summarize content, compare products, or analyze data from any site you're viewing.

## Picks up where you left off

![Browser memory inside ChatGPT](https://images.ctfassets.net/8su2tbn87fck/esiB6cs2iiF20hKkrwLH3/9196a8e8ff40da8a49b8d9c914995e11/Memories__1_.png?q=90&fm=webp)

![Atlas memory icon](https://images.ctfassets.net/8su2tbn87fck/3vLgVYKpNQVaRmU2IeDnkZ/e0a753ff9736e5904c721275ccac8df6/memories-eyebrow__1_.svg?q=90)

You can choose what ChatGPT remembers, so it can bring you relevant details when you need them.

## Takes action for you

![Agents at work inside Atlas](https://images.ctfassets.net/8su2tbn87fck/5GwOd60TcIrrdjjh5ojp1u/e6d980f8643d3ac2fc3aec1d36cae58b/Agent.png?q=90&fm=webp)

![Atlas agent cursor](https://images.ctfassets.net/8su2tbn87fck/6JjdfRcbvXfJ07sj2IgYx4/da2958d4039ca989229c28c0bd78ebcf/Agent-eyebrow.svg?q=90)

In agent mode, ChatGPT interacts with sites for you, always under your control. Use it to do tasks from start to finish, like researching and shopping for a trip. Available in preview for Plus, Pro, and Business accounts.

## Assists you when and where you need it

![Rewrite anything inside Atlas](https://images.ctfassets.net/8su2tbn87fck/BAHk1HiqpZiUYWgNtLTrH/9d9c81d31542122715048a70a01d8537/Frame_1__8_.png?q=90&fm=webp)

![Atlas cursor icon](https://images.ctfassets.net/8su2tbn87fck/7l9ECsl8ByxlqU5HCwtjie/e1909d295e5ce16d32df1e200401df5c/Cursor-collaborator-eyebrow.svg?q=90)

Turn your cursor into a collaborator. Highlight text in emails, calendar invites,  or docs, and get help from chat in one click.

![Privacy](https://images.ctfassets.net/8su2tbn87fck/3x9XCguHDuDW3ukUM9sFh6/121ea40684cec5e8b4943c05828393e5/prviacy.svg?q=90)

## You’re in control

You can decide which sites ChatGPT can see, clear your browsing history, use incognito, and manage browser memories anytime.
