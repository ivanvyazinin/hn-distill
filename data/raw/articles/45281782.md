## How it works

This is a new category of display that combines a fixed holographic etched background with a high resolution 4K screen. It is optimized for presenting people, characters, and products on a fixed 3D stage that makes them feel more real and present. The display creates true visual depth in a razor thin form factor, capturing attention using standard 2D workflows like background removal, shadows, and reflections.

## Service and support

**How does the HLD‚ĄĘ technology work?**

Hololuminescent‚ĄĘ Display technology builds on a decade of holographic and light field experience and intellectual property.. This new HLD technology combines a fixed holographic volume baked into the display's optical stack, creating true depth and dimension that makes people, characters, and products appear to float in space.  
  
The HLD does not require eye-tracking cameras. It is intended to work as well for one person as it does for dozens of people at the same time.

‚ÄĘ 1080p output for the HLD 16"
