## Some Playstations that I bought the other day…

I once wrote a blog post about [Nintendo Wii controllers](https://retrohax.net/shorts-nintendo-wii-controllers-fixes/). That post was already based on old data, and the job presented in this blog post is from roughly the same period of time when I bought around 60 various consoles to learn how to fix them and to show them to my son.

I had around 9 PlayStation 2 units. Seven were the FAT PS2s, and I will be covering only fixes/mods that I find a bit more interesting than the usual clean&run scenario. Obviously, a lot of time passed, and nowadays there are way better mods available, but anyway, let’s go.

## Fixes

PS2 motherboards are well-manufactured and generally free from issues. In my case, all was working fine.

However, I had to clean them nicely, replace the original Sony batteries, and replace thermal pads.

Some pics below.

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210312_170225-1600x1200.jpg)

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210312_174647-1600x1200.jpg)

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210312_174629-1600x1200.jpg)

Replacing thermal pads.

Some drives needed a new laser module. The original is KHS-400C, but replacements are still available.

I’ve bought some on [Aliexpress](https://aliexpress.com/).

Some plastic parts had to be restored to be fully functional. I’ve used acetone, some wire reinforcement, and a soldering iron to fix it.

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210316_135613-1600x1200.jpg)

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210316_135618-1600x1200.jpg)

## Mods

The mods are rather basic and mandatory in modern days, and have also been covered many times on YouTube. It all basically boils down to the installation of [FMCB](https://github.com/israpps/FreeMcBoot-Installer/releases/tag/latest) or FHDB, which is a great solution!

To make it work, I needed some hard drives, memcards, and HDD adapters.

I’ve bought some brand new 1TB SATA drives along with HDD adapters that are easily available. I had a bit of trouble finding nice and original memcards, but that was sorted out, too.

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210322_180159-1600x1200.jpg)

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210324_113704-1600x1200.jpg)

Next, I had to prepare a drive to work nicely with PS2. For testing purposes, I’ve used a 2.5-inch drive in a 3D printed bracket.

Software used back then to format the drive is [Window HDL Image Install Program V1.7.6 By Gadgetfreak](https://sites.google.com/view/contverconsmi)

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210313_102204-1600x1200.jpg)

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210313_102234-1600x1200.jpg)

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210313_102307-1600x1200.jpg)

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210313_102319-1600x1200.jpg)

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210313_102348-1600x1200.jpg)

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210313_102403-1600x1200.jpg)

## Controllers

I had quite a few controllers, but I needed to test them first and fix them if broken.

Having a working PS2 with FMCB or FHDB is handy, as I could upload a [PS2 controller tester by jbit\_](https://www.psx-place.com/resources/ps2-controller-tester-by-jbit.670/) and check all the pads.

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210324_063922-1600x1200.jpg)

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210324_064507-1600x1200.jpg)

Some controllers are originally painted with a rubber-like cover that, unfortunately, degrades with time and becomes a sticky gooey. I usually deal with it with the help of Methanol. It nicely removes it. However, such a cleaned surface has to be painted with an undercoating and the desired color afterward. This is exactly what I did with one set of controllers that I had.

## Finale

Out of the six PS2 consoles, I’ve created 6 sets in the following configuration:

*   PS2 FAT – new RTC battery, new thermal pads, laser module
*   1TB SATA HDD + HDD adapter
*   original 8MB Sony memcard
*   new power wire
*   Controller
*   PS2HDMI dongle

![IMG](https://retrohax.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/IMG_20210326_142901-1600x1200.jpg)

Unfortunately, nobody wanted to buy these even for a cost that would cover all the expenses spent on this project. On the bright side, I have learned a lot, plus, I have a cool PS2 to play with 🙂
