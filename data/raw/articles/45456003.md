## Introducing Handheld XR

PortalVR re-invents XR by removing the need for a headset. It is a fundamentally new way to test, capture, and play XR content.

![Icon 01](/assets/images/features-icon-01.svg)

Handheld

Hold your phone or tablet and glance down as you play, keeping the action in view and your neck relaxed.

![Icon 02](/assets/images/features-icon-02.svg)

Phone Controller

Any iOS or Android phone with ARCore or ARKit can be used as a fully tracked VR controller, with all axis controls and buttons in-reach.

![Icon 03](/assets/images/features-icon-03.svg)

Feel

Ultra-low latency controls keep handheld play feel snappy and smooth, making games fully playable.

![Icon 04](/assets/images/features-icon-04.svg)

No Code Changes

Ship the same APK you already build for VR headsets and PortalVR just works—no porting, no patches.

## FAQs

#### What is PortalVR?

PortalVR is a modified version of the [Monado](https://monado.dev/) OpenXR runtime that lets you run compatible XR games and apps on your Android tablet or phone. Use any phone—iOS or Android—as a controller to play installed or sideloaded titles as-is.

#### What hardware do I need?

You need at least one modern Android device to run games and any ARCore- or ARKit-compatible phone for the controller. Most current phones (iOS or Android) work great.

#### What accessories do I need?

If you're using a tablet or a larger phone, a [pop socket](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B07P29XQR4) makes it easier to hold, and [green-magenta 3D glasses](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B005UZB7KM/?coliid=I253XSK343F8BE&colid=21O2BQ1R6YU4W&psc=1&ref_=list_c_wl_lv_ov_lig_dp_it) can be worn for full stereo 3D. (It's pretty cool.) Check out the [PortalVR Essential Gear](https://www.amazon.com/hz/wishlist/ls/21O2BQ1R6YU4W) list.

#### How do I use it?

Open this site on an Android device and follow the download link at the top. For setup guidance and tips, see [the documentation](/docs).

#### What's the difference between free and paid?

The free version keeps a watermark on screen and limits sideloaded game recording to 10 seconds. Otherwise, it has every feature of paid and plays all XR content.

#### How do I get content onto my device?

PortalVR ships with a library of free XR experiences and the Wolvic WebXR browser, so you can launch built-in demos or explore any WebXR app on the internet.

#### What about my own game?

Build an APK in Unity or Unreal, install it on your Android device, and it runs in PortalVR with the same controls you expect on a headset. For help getting your app working, see [the documentation](/docs)

#### What should I use this for?

Developers can test builds without a headset, give walkthrough demos anywhere, and capture smooth flythrough footage for tutorials or social posts.
