### Using reMarkable Paper Pro Move

* * *

*   Can I use Marker for reMarkable 2 with reMarkable Paper Pro Move?
    
    Unfortunately not. Marker and Marker Plus for reMarkable 2 use different technology than Marker and Marker Plus for reMarkable Paper Pro and reMarkable Paper Pro Move. For more details, see our [compatibility guide](https://support.remarkable.com/s/article/Accessories-for-reMarkable-paper-tablets).
    
*   Can I transfer my files from another paper tablet to reMarkable Paper Pro Move?
    
    Yes. When pairing your new paper tablet at [my.remarkable.com](https://my.remarkable.com), you’ll be given the option to transfer and sync all your notes and documents from the cloud.
    
    If you subscribe to our Connect subscription service, you’ll be able to keep using up to three devices on the same reMarkable account. [Learn more about Connect here.](/shop/connect)
    
*   Can I access my notes and documents from my computer or phone?
    
    Yes, with the reMarkable [mobile](https://support.remarkable.com/s/article/Mobile-app) and [desktop](https://support.remarkable.com/s/article/Desktop-app) apps, you can view, organize, and import files. If you subscribe to Connect, you can also take and edit notes in the apps. [Learn more about Connect here](/shop/connect).
    
*   How do I adjust the reading light?
    
    Changing the brightness is easy using the quick settings menu. Tap the battery icon or swipe down from the upper right corner of the display to open quick settings, then use the slider to adjust the strength of the reading light to your environment.
    
*   Does the reading light affect the battery life?
    
    reMarkable Paper Pro Move offers up to two weeks of battery life, but keeping the reading light at maximum brightness will reduce this figure. You won’t need to use the reading light in normal lighting conditions, though. The display reflects natural light, just like paper.
    
*   What is the difference between reMarkable Paper Pro Move and other paper tablets?
    
    reMarkable Paper Pro Move and reMarkable Paper Pro both have color displays (7.3” and 11.8”, respectively), a reading light, and our most paper-like writing feel. reMarkable 2 doesn’t have a reading light, and it has a black-and-white display. You can see a [side-by-side comparison here](/shop/compare).
    
*   Can I write typed text on reMarkable Paper Pro Move without a keyboard?
    
    Yes, you can use the on-screen keyboard to add structure to your handwritten notes or make quick edits to converted text. If you plan to type longer texts on your paper tablet, consider [reMarkable Paper Pro](/products/remarkable-paper/pro) and [Type Folio](/products/remarkable-paper/pro/type-folio).
