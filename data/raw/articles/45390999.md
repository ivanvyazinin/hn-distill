## Beschreibung

Get a focused daily press review in 5 minutes with facts, multiple perspectives  
No doomscrolling, no clickbait, just quality news that respects your time and intelligence.  
  
What You Get:  
• One thoughtful press review per day (updated 12:00 UTC)  
• Coverage: Any and all topics usually covered in the press  
• Multiple perspectives from global sources  
• One-tap access to original articles  
• Clean sections: Summary, Highlights, Key Quotes, Timeline, Context, Impact  
  
How It Works:  
Kagi News reads thousands of community-curated RSS feeds worldwide and distills them into your daily press review. Every item cites sources so you can dive deeper with one tap.  
  
Community Contributions:  
• Contribute sources to broaden perspectives  
• Open source platform with community-curated feeds  
  
Privacy by Design:  
No ads. No trackers. Your attention belongs to you.  
\- https://kagi.com/privacy  
  
Download now for your daily dose of quality journalism without the noise.
