Scryer Prolog Meetup 2025

Do, 13.11.2025  
↳ bis bis Fr, 14.11.2025

Hochschule Düsseldorf  
Gebäude 6

[→ Veranstaltung teilen](https://hsd-pbsa.de/veranstaltung/scryer-prolog-meetup-2025/)

The 3rd Scryer Prolog Meetup will take place on Nov. 13th and 14th 2025 at the Hochschule Düsseldorf in Düsseldorf, Germany.

This meetup is an excellent opportunity to learn more about the latest developments and applications of Scryer Prolog, a modern free ISO compliant Prolog system.

Attendance is free, registration is not required.

If you want to give a talk, please contact [Prof. Christian Jendreiko](mailto:christian.jendreiko@hs-duesseldorf.de) and [Markus Triska](mailto:markus.triska@bka.gv.at).

We hope to see you at the meetup!
