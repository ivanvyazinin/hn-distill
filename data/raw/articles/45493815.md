The basic situation is that if OpenAI announces a big partnership with a public company, that company’s stock will go up. Today OpenAI [announced](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/2488/000119312525230895/d28189dex991.htm) a deal to buy tens of billions of dollars of chips from Advanced Micro Devices Inc., and AMD’s stock went up. As of noon today, AMD’s stock was at $213 per share, up about 29% from Friday’s close; it had added about $78 billion of market capitalization.
