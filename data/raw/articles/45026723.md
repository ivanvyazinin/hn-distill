## How it works

Think of it as an adblocker for your email. Install carefully crafted filters and watch your inbox transform.

*   **Install once** - Download and import the filter collection to your email client
    
*   **Automatic organization** - Promotional emails are moved to ‘Archive’ folder automatically
    
*   **Main inbox stays clean** - Only personal emails, important notifications, and urgent emails remain
    
*   **Never miss important emails** - The filters are designed to be conservative and only move obvious promotional content
    
*   **It’s your inbox** - Delete or change any filter. It’s your email!
    
*   **Open sourced** - Community-maintained filters that improve over time
    
*   **No apps or installs** - Free to use, just filters, so no apps to install or authorize

## FAQs

How do these email filters work?

You download and import a collection of smart email filters into your Gmail or Fastmail account. These filters automatically identify promotional emails and move them to an ‘Archive’ folder, keeping only important personal emails in your main inbox.

Where did my promotional emails go?

Promotional emails are automatically moved to your ‘Archive’ folder or ‘\_Archive’ label. Nothing is deleted - everything is preserved and searchable. You can still access these emails anytime, they just won’t clutter your main inbox or trigger notifications.

Is this safe and private?

This runs in your email account. These are standard email filters that run ONLY within your email provider (Gmail/Fastmail). This organizes your existing emails using your email provider’s built-in filtering system.

Will this work on my mobile device?

Yes! Once you install the filters on your email account, they work everywhere - desktop, mobile app, web interface. The filtering happens at the server level, so all your devices will see the same organized inbox.

What if an important email gets filtered?

The filters are designed to be conservative and move obvious promotional emails. If an important email does get archived, you can easily find it in your Archive folder and create a custom filter to ensure it stays in your inbox in the future.

How much does this cost?

It’s free! This is an open source project. Download the filters and use them forever. Or delete them. No subscriptions or accounts to create.

How do I find my emails?

A folder or label is created called ‘Archive’ they are under that folder or label.
