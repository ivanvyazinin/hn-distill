# The Ironclad Operating System Kernel

Ironclad is a [formally verified](/formalverification.html), real-time capable, UNIX-like operating system kernel for general-purpose and embedded uses. It is written in [SPARK](https://www.adacore.com/about-spark) and [Ada](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ada_\(programming_language\)), and is comprised of 100% free software.

Ironclad features a familiar [POSIX](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/POSIX)\-compatible interface, true simultaneous preemptive multitasking, [Mandatory Access Control (MAC)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mandatory_access_control), and support for hard real-time scheduling.

## Why Choose Ironclad?

## ![](/icons/foss.svg)Free as in freedom

Ironclad is fully open source and distributed under the [GPLv3](https://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-3.0.en.html), ensuring it remains free. No firmware blobs are needed or shipped with the kernel. Every piece of the stack is open source.

## ![](/icons/formalverification.svg)Formal verification

[SPARK](https://www.adacore.com/about-spark)'s state of the art [formal verification](/formalverification.html) is employed for ensuring absence of errors and correctness of huge swathes of Ironclad, like cryptography, [MAC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mandatory_access_control), and user-facing facilities.

## ![](/icons/portable.svg)Portable

Ported to several platforms and boards, and designed to be easily portable to many more. Dependency on only the GNU toolchain allows for easy cross-compilation.

## ![](/icons/ecosystem.svg)Distributions and ecosystem

[POSIX](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/POSIX) compatibility makes software porting and development easy. The project features distributions ready for download and use for all the available architectures and boards, the most prominent FOSS one is [Gloire](https://codeberg.org/Ironclad/Gloire).

## Who pays for Ironclad?

**Ironclad will always be free for use, study, and modification**, so, to support the project, we rely on the use of donations and grants. Every contribution makes a difference and allows us to do more.
