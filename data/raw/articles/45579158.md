# Title:How Gravity Can Explain the Collapse of the Wavefunction

Authors:[Sabine Hossenfelder](https://arxiv.org/search/quant-ph?searchtype=author&query=Hossenfelder,+S)

View a PDF of the paper titled How Gravity Can Explain the Collapse of the Wavefunction, by Sabine Hossenfelder

[View PDF](/pdf/2510.11037) [HTML (experimental)](https://arxiv.org/html/2510.11037v1)

> Abstract:I present a simple argument for why a fundamental theory that unifies matter and gravity gives rise to what seems to be a collapse of the wavefunction. The resulting model is local, parameter-free and makes testable predictions.
