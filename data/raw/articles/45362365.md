## Description

Experience the world’s first motorcycle racing game controlled with Earbuds! Tilt your head, dodge traffic, ride with speed.  
  
RidePods – The World’s First Earbuds-Controlled Motorcycle Racing Game  
  
RidePods brings a brand-new way to experience motorcycle racing. Instead of tapping buttons, you control your motorbike with your head movements using your earbuds’ motion sensors. Tilt left or right to steer, dodge cars, chase high scores, and feel the thrill of fast-paced arcade racing like never before.  
  
Whether you are a casual rider or a competitive racer, RidePods offers an addictive gameplay loop that keeps you coming back. Race through endless roads, beat your own records, and push your limits with every ride.  
  
Features:  
  
Innovative earbuds-controlled gameplay – tilt your head to steer your motorcycle.  
  
Fast-paced arcade racing with smooth controls and responsive handling.  
  
Dodge cars, trucks, and obstacles while chasing the highest score.  
  
Compete against yourself: beat your best run and climb higher.  
  
Simple, clean design optimized for quick play sessions.  
  
Perfect for fans of motorcycle games, motorbike racing, and arcade riders, RidePods is designed to test your reflexes and immerse you in the thrill of the ride.  
  
Can you master the road and become the ultimate rider? Download RidePods now and race with your head movements!  
  
  
Note: RidePods requires supported earbuds with motion sensors (such as AirPods Pro or AirPods 3rd Generation) for head-movement controls. Without compatible earbuds, gameplay may not function as intended.
