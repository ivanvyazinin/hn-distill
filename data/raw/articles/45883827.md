# DEC Mini

* * *

A 3D-printable, fully working, customizable computer inspired by one of the loveliest retro computers of the 80s

![](https://decmini.tin.cat/img/dec-mini-photo-1.jpg)

The long-awaited new **DEC Mini** is the latest iteration of the DEC[1](#fn:1) business computers that set an industry standard starting with the [VT-series terminals](https://www.simplyeighties.com/dec-terminals.php) in the 70s.

Conceived to satisfy the most demanding users both at the office and at home, the **DEC Mini** owes its power to multiple state-of-the-art parallel processors, is fully IBM-PC compatible, offers extreme upgradability and packs unprecedented high-end specifications. With a small form factor that seamlessly fits in your desktop, the **DEC Mini** truly is destined to revolutionize the microcomputer industry of the decade:

*   Up to 4 processors at 1.500 Mhz [2](#fn:2)
*   Up to 8.192 Megabytes of RAM [2](#fn:2)
*   Low-noise hard disk with up to 65.536 Megabytes [2](#fn:2)
*   64 bit architecture, retrocompatible with 8, 16 and 32 bit systems [3](#fn:3)
*   3½ inch. High Density 1.44Mb disk drive
*   Full DOS, Windows and Macintosh compatibility [3](#fn:3)
*   SVGA color display 1024x768. VGA/EGA/CGA/Hercules compatible
*   Integrated wide range stereo sound, Sound Blaster 16 fully compatible
*   Integrated high quality keyboard
*   Affordable price

## Choose between a wide range of motherboards

The DEC Mini can be fitted with most of the currently existing SOC computer boards including the [Raspberry Pi](https://www.raspberrypi.org), [LattePanda](https://www.lattepanda.com), [Udoo Volt](https://shop.udoo.org), [Banana Pi](http://www.banana-pi.org) and much more, allowing for the widest range of computing and performance choices in the industry.

## Extreme compatibility

Thanks to a complete support of the new USB connectivity industry standard, the DEC Mini is compatible with the whole range of currently available USB peripherals, including mice, dot matrix printers and high speed modems. The DEC Mini is capable of running Linux, DOS, Windows and can even replace your existing Commodore 64 workstation[3](#fn:3).

## Extreme connectivity

Built in industry standard RJ-45 socket allows the DEC Mini to connect to most modern local area networks at up to 1.073.741.824 baud [2](#fn:2). By additionally supporting the upcoming Wi-Fi and Bluetooth technologies for wire-less connections, your **DEC Mini** is guaranteed to be future-ready, and will be for years to come.

![](https://decmini.tin.cat/img/dec-mini-2.jpg)

## Colorful choices

Choose the classic color combination, or let the **DEC Mini** fit your life style with the **Cherry gum pink**, the **Springfield family blue**, the limited edition **Tropper black**, the **Surfer sunset orange** or the **Gentle hacker purple** colors.
