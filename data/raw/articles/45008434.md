## NVIDIA DGX Spark

## Workloads

## Accelerate All AI Workloads

Delivering the power of an AI supercomputer in a desktop-friendly size, NVIDIA DGX Spark is ideal for AI developer, researcher, and data scientist workloads.

#### Prototyping

Develop, test, and validate AI models and applications.

With the NVIDIA AI software stack, NVIDIA DGX Spark provides the platform for developers to create, test, and validate AI models and AI-augmented applications and solutions. For final tuning or deployment, easily migrate work to NVIDIA DGX cloud or other NVIDIA accelerated data centers or cloud infrastructures.

    ![Prototyping](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAEAAAABCAQAAAC1HAwCAAAAC0lEQVR42mNkYAAAAAYAAjCB0C8AAAAASUVORK5CYII= "Prototyping")

#### Fine-Tuning

Fine-tune AI models up to 70 billion parameters.

Improve the performance of pre-trained models by fine-tuning on NVIDIA DGX Spark. With 128GB of unified system memory, fine-tune models up to 70 billion parameters to customize AI models and solutions for specific needs and use cases.

    ![Fine-Tuning](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAEAAAABCAQAAAC1HAwCAAAAC0lEQVR42mNkYAAAAAYAAjCB0C8AAAAASUVORK5CYII= "Fine-Tuning")

#### Inference

Test, validate, and inference with AI models up to 200 billion parameters.

Fifth-generation Tensor Cores with support for FP4 deliver up to 1,000 TOPS of AI computing performance, combined with 128GB of system memory, accelerate inference of [state-of-the-art AI models](http://build.nvidia.com) to test, validate and deploy from your NVIDIA DGX Spark.

    ![Inference](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAEAAAABCAQAAAC1HAwCAAAAC0lEQVR42mNkYAAAAAYAAjCB0C8AAAAASUVORK5CYII= "Inference")

#### Data Science

Accelerate end-to-end data science workflows with [NVIDIA RAPIDS](https://rapids.ai)™.

From data preparation to model training, inference, and deployment, NVIDIA RAPIDS on NVIDIA DGX Spark simplifies the development process with Zero-code-change accelerators and familiar APIs to quickly accelerate existing workloads or rapidly develop specific use-case solutions, and easily scale out to data center or cloud.

    ![Data Science](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAEAAAABCAQAAAC1HAwCAAAAC0lEQVR42mNkYAAAAAYAAjCB0C8AAAAASUVORK5CYII= "Data Science")

#### Edge Applications

Develop edge applications with NVIDIA AI frameworks, including Isaac™, Metropolis, and many others.

DGX Spark provides an excellent platform for developing robotics, smart cities, and computer vision solutions. NVIDIA frameworks, including [Isaac](https://developer.nvidia.com/isaac), [Metropolis](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/autonomous-machines/intelligent-video-analytics-platform/), and [Holoscan](https://developer.nvidia.com/holoscan-sdk),. These frameworks and tools enable developers to take advantage of the power of NVIDIA DGX Spark to quickly develop edge applications.

    ![Edge Applications](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAEAAAABCAQAAAC1HAwCAAAAC0lEQVR42mNkYAAAAAYAAjCB0C8AAAAASUVORK5CYII= "Edge Applications")
