# World’s highest bridge unveiled at more than 2,000ft above ground

The world’s tallest bridge, which stands at more than 2,000ft above ground, has officially opened in [China](/topic/china).

Drone footage shared by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs on Sunday (27 September) shows the newly opened Huajiang Grand Canyon Bridge, which stands above a gorge in the Guizhou province. Underneath the structure, a rainbow can be seen thanks to a “spectacular water curtain test”.

The 2,890-metre-long structure, which took more than three years to complete, reduces travel time between the two sides of the canyon from two hours to two minutes.

Last month, a team of engineers deployed 96 trucks to strategic points across the bridge to recreate heavy traffic conditions to ensure it would not buckle.[](https://www.ndtv.com/beeps/world-news/watch-worlds-tallest-bridge-opens-in-china-cuts-travel-time-from-2-hour-to-2-minutes-9362794?pfrom=quick_read_beeps)[](https://vdo.ai/contact?utm_medium=video&utm_term=ndtv.com&utm_source=vdoai_logo)
