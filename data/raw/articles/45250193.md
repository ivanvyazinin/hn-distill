# Daffodil - Storefront that can connect to any ecommerce backend.

[](#daffodil---storefront-that-can-connect-to-any-ecommerce-backend)

[![daffodil-logo](/graycoreio/daffodil/raw/develop/apps/daffio/src/assets/appicons/icon-384x384.png)](/graycoreio/daffodil/blob/develop/apps/daffio/src/assets/appicons/icon-384x384.png)  
_Daffodil is an ecommerce development framework for building high-quality storefronts that can connect to any ecommerce backend._  

[**daff.io**](https://www.daff.io/) | [**demo.daff.io**](https://demo.daff.io/)
