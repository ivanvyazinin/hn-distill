# Investing in America 2025

10 articles

articles 10

10 articles

articles 10

Google's investments across the U.S are helping enable this extraordinary time for American innovation. Through major investments in technical infrastructure, research and development — along with [expanded energy capacity](https://blog.google/outreach-initiatives/public-policy/pennsylvania-energy-innovation-summit/) for an AI-driven economy and [workforce development](https://blog.google/outreach-initiatives/google-org/electrical-workers-ai-training/) and [education programs](https://blog.google/outreach-initiatives/grow-with-google/virginia-ai-scholarships-training/) — we will help the U.S. continue to lead the world in AI. These investments also unlock substantial economic opportunity for American businesses — advancing scientific breakthroughs, fortifying cybersecurity for the U.S., and creating new career opportunities for millions of Americans.

In recent months, we’ve been bringing this new era of American innovation to life in communities across the country, with more to come.
