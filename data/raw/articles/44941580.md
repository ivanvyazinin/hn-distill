### Simplicity

All in one package, and two main instructions: just upload the media you want to analyze, and check the results.

### Flexibility

Choose between Python, Typescript, Java, Go, and Rust.

### Transparency

All variants are available as open source.

### Accessibility

Any developer can access 50 audio or image scans per month with the new free tier.

## Get Deepfake Detection Where You Need It Most

*   Identity verification
    
    Detect AI-generated faces or voices for KYC, onboarding, or secure access.
    
*   Fraud detection
    
    Spot deepfake impersonations used for scams, account takeovers, or social engineering.
    
*   Content moderation
    
    Flag AI-generated media on social or community platforms
    
*   Synthetic media detection
    
    Detect AI-manipulated media; not exclusive to human faces or voices
    
*   Media analysis and fact-checking
    
    Detect AI-manipulated media in journalism, OSINT, or public intelligence work.
    
*   Research and testing
    
    Experiment with detection models or benchmark against other tools.
