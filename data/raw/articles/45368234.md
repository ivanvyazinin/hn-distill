I recently learned about binturongs, ridiculous animals which look like a hybrid of roughly five different cute critters, galumph about the place, and [smell suspiciously like popcorn](https://www.nationalgeographic.com/science/article/why-your-popcorn-smells-like-a-bearcats-butt)[1](#sn-body-1). Thank you to the algorithmic Youtube overlords for blessing me with the above video.
