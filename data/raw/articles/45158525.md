# cloud-to-butt

[](#cloud-to-butt)

[![](/panicsteve/cloud-to-butt/raw/master/logo.png)](/panicsteve/cloud-to-butt/blob/master/logo.png)

Chrome extension that replaces occurrences of 'the cloud' with 'my butt'

[Direct download of crx file](https://github.com/panicsteve/cloud-to-butt/blob/master/CloudToButt.crx?raw=true)

Note that there are forks of this extension that simply replace 'cloud' with 'butt'. In my personal opinion, that approach is too broad and it's less funny as a result, but it is clearly a very polarizing issue in the cloud-to-butt user community. Forks are free to do whatever they like. But officially, _this_ extension replaces only the phrase described above, and therefore it did not replace your cloudflare URLs with buttflare URLs. Thank you for your concern.
