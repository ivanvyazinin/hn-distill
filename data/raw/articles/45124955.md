# Google Down in Eastern Europe (UPDATED)

[World](articles/category/30/World) | September 4, 2025, Thursday // 10:22

*   [Tweet](https://twitter.com/share?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw)

![Bulgaria: Google Down in Eastern Europe (UPDATED)](https://www.novinite.com/media/images/2025-09/photo_verybig_234225.jpg)

Users across multiple Eastern European countries reported a significant and ongoing outage affecting a suite of **Google** services, causing widespread disruption to both work and daily life.

> [#BREAKING](https://twitter.com/hashtag/BREAKING?src=hash&ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw) **Google** services **down** in some countries, primarily felt across Southeastern **Europe** [pic.twitter.com/cMYRYPHFi8](https://t.co/cMYRYPHFi8)
> 
> — Anadolu English (@anadoluagency) [September 4, 2025](https://twitter.com/anadoluagency/status/1963505253518581854?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw)

Reports began flooding into downdetector.com and social media platforms around from users in Bulgaria, Turkey, Greece and other Eastern European countries. The issues appear to be widespread and are affecting core **Google** products.

> **Google** **down** in the Caucasus, Turkey, and the Balkans [pic.twitter.com/E2SHOuKOA9](https://t.co/E2SHOuKOA9)
> 
> — Hov Nazaretyan (@HovhanNaz) [September 4, 2025](https://twitter.com/HovhanNaz/status/1963505155363512687?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw)

**Which Services Were Impacted?**

The outage did not seem to be universal for all **Google** services, but the affected apps were critical to many:

*   YouTube: Users experienced an inability to load videos, with many seeing error messages or an endless loading loop. Both the website and mobile app were affected.
*   **Google** Maps: The service was failing to load map data, search for locations, or calculate routes, leaving travellers and commuters without navigation assistance.
*   **Google** Search: In a particularly impactful failure, the core **Google** Search engine was returning error messages or failing to complete searches for a significant number of users.
*   Gmail: Some users were reporting issues with sending and receiving emails, though this appeared to be less consistent than the other outages.
*   **Google** Drive: Access to cloud-stored documents and files was also disrupted for many.

The common thread among error messages was a "_5xx server error_" – a type of error that indicates a problem on **Google**'s end, not with the user's individual internet connection.

Alternatively, users can use other search engines such as [Bing](https://www.bing.com/hp), [Yahoo](https://search.yahoo.com/), [DuckDuckGo](https://duckduckgo.com), and [Brave Search](https://search.brave.com/).

_**This is a developing story. We will update this article with more information as it becomes available, including an official response from Google.**_
