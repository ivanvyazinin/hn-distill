# Title:Asymptotically optimal approximate Hadamard matrices

Authors:[Boris Alexeev](https://arxiv.org/search/math?searchtype=author&query=Alexeev,+B), [John Jasper](https://arxiv.org/search/math?searchtype=author&query=Jasper,+J), [Dustin G. Mixon](https://arxiv.org/search/math?searchtype=author&query=Mixon,+D+G)

View a PDF of the paper titled Asymptotically optimal approximate Hadamard matrices, by Boris Alexeev and 2 other authors

[View PDF](/pdf/2511.14653) [HTML (experimental)](https://arxiv.org/html/2511.14653v1)

> Abstract:In this paper, we study approximate Hadamard matrices, that is, well-conditioned $n\\times n$ matrices with all entries in $\\{\\pm1\\}$. We show that the smallest-possible condition number goes to $1$ as $n\\to\\infty$, and we identify some explicit infinite families of approximate Hadamard matrices.
