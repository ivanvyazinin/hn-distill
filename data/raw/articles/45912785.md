**LINE Seed is LINE’s new typeface that was  
created based on the brand's convenient  
usability and friendly identity.  
Just like a seed  
takes root and bears fruit, the name Seed  
means to take root firmly in LINE's services  
and grow together  
with our users.  
All the fonts of LINE Seed have the same  
DNA, and the balanced volume and weight  
ensures a harmony  
without requiring extra  
adjustments so that the text’s texture looks  
the same even when different languages  
are put side by side.**

![Our Voice](./src/images/en/msg_title01.png)

LINE has been conducting global business in various fields around the world. LINE Seed was created in four writing systems  
starting with Latin, Korean, Japanese, and Thai so that it can be used universally throughout its global network.  
As a result, we can now deliver our message in a consistent voice.

![Our Design](./src/images/en/msg_title02.png)

LINE Seed is a Geometric typeface with some Gothic influences and an emphasis on simplicity. It’s designed to look familiar  
to everyone in a variety of places. The LINE logo’s straight form has been maintained while also emphasizing clear curves,  
resulting in a functional typeface with great readability, usability, and legibility.

![Our Identity](./src/images/en/msg_title03.png)

The value of LINE aims to make the lives of users more convenient through seamless connection with everything  
including people, information, content, and online and offline services—anytime and anywhere.  
This value is what sets LINE Seed apart from other typefaces.

![](./src/images/en/square_tit_pc.png) ![](./src/images/en/square_tit_mo.png)

With the ligature OpenType feature, LINE Seed is able to maintain  
a consistent letter spacing  
when certain characters (fi, ff, fl, fj, etc.)  
are arranged side by side. This is achieved by connecting  
or transforming  
characters to improve the counter space (inner white space), which  
achieves  
a better rhythm from Display to Text settings.  
  
In addition, the various service icons of LINE are  
included in the text, adding visual fun through clever expressions.

to our global users. The typeface was designed based on typographical elements as  
well as morphological elements in order to be read in a unified and natural way while  
maintaining the unique form and function of the characters in each writing system.
