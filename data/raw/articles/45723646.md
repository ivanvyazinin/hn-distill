# Dlog Model

###### Proprietary Model

## How personality and character drive well-being and income

### **The Dlog model is the backbone of the quantitative and qualitative analysis.**

Dlog’s private AI model is based on our lead scientist’s PhD and bolstered by decades of research into Productivity and Well-Being, mapping your life across the four constructs – Personality, Character, Resources and Well-Being.

When you first use the coach, you answer a baseline survey of the four constructs, so that Dlog knows where you have been over the past year. As you journal and work on projects, your entries are scored against the same baseline survey so that you can see if you are above or below your norm in any of the four rings.

From there, Dlog’s AI suggests next steps that you can choose from in order to maximise your personal Productivity and Well-Being.

![](https://dlog.pro/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/Untitled-design-9.gif)

#### Through regular, guided journaling and accountable projects, Dlog helps you achieve your goals with an AI coach informed by your personal data analysed through the Dlog Model.

Regressions and structural equation modelling (SEM) are then used to trace directed paths between constructs, showing how changes in one area drives changes in another (e.g., habits, people or traits influencing mood, progress or resources). Dlog focuses on the most informative positive and negative paths, and includes a Quality of Well-Being dimension to distinguish hollow wins from integrated fulfilment.

Numbers are never shown without context. Trends and relationships are paired with diary quotes, bringing you back to the moment and grounding the insights in you.
