# Flowers of the Sky

![comet](https://pdr-assets.b-cdn.net/collections/flowers-of-the-sky/15155837078_0c1edf1eeb_o.jpg?width=1200&height=850)

Augsburger Wunderzeichenbuch, Folio 28, c. 1552 — [Source](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Augsburger_Wunderzeichenbuch,_Folio_28.jpg)

[](#p-0-0)

Depictions spanning almost a whole millennium - in chronological order - of comets, meteors, meteorites and shooting stars.
