# [I went to prison](/)

[Home](/) [Website](/website/) [Charges](/charges/) [Prison](/prison/)

This is an informational website about why I went to prison, and my prison experience. In short, I had a sports streaming website from 2016-2021.

I self-surrendered to FCI Thomson in January 2024, and was released about 18 months later in August of 2025.

Most of the websites that carried information about my charges or my sentencing were inaccurate at best. The TorrentFreak articles were probably the most accurate. I hope to provide some much-needed context.
