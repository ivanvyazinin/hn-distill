# Sandisk launches dongle-like Extreme Fit USB-C flash drive with up to 1 TB capacity

[![The Extreme Fit starts at $15.99. Pictured: a promo picture of the flash drive. (Image source: Sandisk)](fileadmin/_processed_/2/1/csm_Sandisk-launches-Extreme-Fit-USB-C-flash-drive_d7ad0a27fb.jpg)](fileadmin/Notebooks/News/_nc5/Sandisk-launches-Extreme-Fit-USB-C-flash-drive.jpg)

The Extreme Fit starts at $15.99. Pictured: a promo picture of the flash drive. (Image source: Sandisk)

The Extreme Fit has been formally announced, and this dongle-like USB Type-C flash drive is available with up to 1 TB capacity. Sandisk also notes that it delivers USB 3.2 Gen 1 performance, and the starting launch price is $15.99.

Sandisk has introduced the Extreme Fit USB-C flash drive, and this compact storage solution starts at $15.99. Unlike traditional pen drive models, the company has opted for a design that's similar to the dongles of wireless mice and keyboards. Such a design makes the drive not stick out much from the port when connected to a device.

As for the performance, Sandisk is promising USB 3.2 Gen 1 speed. That is, the Type-C flash drive can offer a read speed of up to 400 MB/s, but this rating is only applicable for the options starting from 128 GB. As for the base offering with 64 GB of storage, it has a rated read speed of up to 300 MB/s, which is also a decent transfer speed given that it's not an external SSD (1 TB Samsung T9 curr. [$119.99 on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/SAMSUNG-Portable-Professionals-MU-PG1T0B-AM/dp/B0CHFSWM2P?tag=nbcnewsnet-20)).

Speaking of which, while the Sandisk Extreme Fit is small in size, it's available with up to 1 TB of storage. The USB-C flash drive is also said to be compatible with multiple platforms, including [iPadOS](True-multitasking-and-menu-bar-for-the-Apple-iPad-iPadOS-26-now-available.1115207.0.html). The brand further highlights that due to the size, it's not only a plug-and-play flash drive but also a "plug-and-stay" storage solution.

Other notable highlights include a sleek design, 3 grams of weight, and compatibility with the Sandisk Memory Zone app. The USB-C flash drive is available [on the official store](https://shop.sandisk.com/products/usb-flash-drives/sandisk-extreme-fit-usb-c?sku=SDCZ530-1T00-G46) in the following options:

*   64 GB (curr. $14.99 instead of $15.99)
*   128 GB (curr. $19.99 instead of $21.99)
*   256 GB (curr. $27.99 instead of $29.99)
*   512 GB (curr. $54.99 instead of $59.99)
*   1 TB (curr. $109.99 instead of $117.99)
