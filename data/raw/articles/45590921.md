## Features

AI-powered organization with smart integrations for every content type

## How It Works

Three steps: Share, Process, Organize

### 1\. Share Content

Share reels, shorts, and videos from Instagram, YouTube, or other platforms directly to Trott

### 2\. AI Processing

Automatic extraction of locations, recipes, steps, or product details based on content type using LLMs

### 3\. Search & Use

Fuzzy search, AI chat, and smart integrations—maps for travel, recipe cards for cooking, tutorials for learning
