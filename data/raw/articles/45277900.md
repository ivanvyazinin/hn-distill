# A 3D-Printed Business Card Embosser

## By product designer Igor Daemen

By [Rain Noe](https://codex.core77.com/users/Rain-Noe) - September 9

in [Digital Fabrication](https://www.core77.com/Digital-Fabrication?utm_source=from_tags)

This 3D-printed contraption is by [Igor Daemen](https://www.instagram.com/makerspace.online/?g=5), an Eindhoven-based product designer. "I designed this businesscard embosser to be modular and 3D printable without using any support and without any hardware required to assemble," he writes.

![](https://s3files.core77.com/blog/images/1754670_81_138492_yWXLqbaZ3.png)

![](https://s3files.core77.com/blog/images/1754667_81_138492__WAEsfu2o.png)

![](https://s3files.core77.com/blog/images/1754666_81_138492_MHW7LgKw_.jpg)

"The tolerances are tight," Daemen explains. "And some materials work better then others. I have had best results using Basic PLA."

![](https://s3files.core77.com/blog/images/1754672_81_138492_EcV4YlzqF.jpg)

![](https://s3files.core77.com/blog/images/1754673_81_138492_RlTpThP3v.jpg)

![](https://s3files.core77.com/blog/images/1754668_81_138492_OCUlnp8dC.png)

You can download the files for free [here](https://makerworld.com/en/models/148536-business-card-embosser-level-up-your-cards#profileId-162074).
