## Daily # of Discussions in Cursor Support Forum

This dashboard shows the 30-day moving average of daily discussion posts about Cursor in their [support forum](https://forum.cursor.com/). Data was taken from calling the Discourse API to get all posts from the past to today.

Notes:

1.  Visual Studio Marketplace shows the total installs for an extension. I simply took the total installs at the end of the day, and subtracted it from the total installs at the beginning of the day, for each day.
2.  Discourse API lets you paginate through all of the posts in Cursor's support forum starting from the most recent: [https://forum.cursor.com/latest.json?no\_definitions=true&order=default&page=0](https://forum.cursor.com/latest.json?no_definitions=true&order=default&page=0)
