# Committing to a better future

Lend your skills to an open source project focused on the [Digital Public Goods (DPGs)](https://digitalpublicgoods.net/digital-public-goods/). From fighting climate change, to solving world hunger, your efforts will contribute to creating a better future for everyone. Together, we can drive positive and lasting contributions to the world, one commit at a time.
