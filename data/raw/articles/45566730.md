## Feature Highlights

### Interactive Lessons

Structured 5 to 10 screens lessons with interactive visualizations and audio playback.

![](https://cadenceguitar.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/iphone_lesson-498x1080.png)

### Intuitive recaps

Single page flashcards that condense all the lesson content for quick review.

![](https://cadenceguitar.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/iphone_recap-1-498x1080.png)

### Playful Challenges

Theory, visual and audio based challenges with increasing difficulty and trophies to get even the most dopamine-addicted mind to work.

![](https://cadenceguitar.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/challenge2-2-498x1080.png)

### Ear training

Sound-backed lessons and dedicated audio challenges to recognize intervals, chords, scales, and progressions by ear.

![](https://cadenceguitar.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/audio2-2-498x1080.png)

### Progress Tracking

Daily activity report, streaks and global completion status, helping you stay motivated and focused on your goals.

![](https://cadenceguitar.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/iphone_activity-498x1080.png)

### Complete Guitar Library

A vast collection of 2000+ chords, scales including CAGED, 3NPS, octaves, arpeggios in various positions, and progressions with voicing suggestions.

![](https://cadenceguitar.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/iphone_library-498x1080.png)
