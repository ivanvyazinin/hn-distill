Thought you would be interested to know that Marius Gerbershagen has used ecl to compile Maxima to wasm to run in a browser. I believe it's a pretty complete implementation with fancy TeX display of formulas and nice graphics via gnuplot compiled to wasm. See it at [http://maxima-on-wasm.pages.dev/](http://maxima-on-wasm.pages.dev/) -- Ray
