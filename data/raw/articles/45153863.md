## KindnessSender Guidelines

×

### Our Mission

KindnessSender exists to spread genuine kindness, support, and positivity to people who need it most. Every message should uplift, encourage, and bring light to someone's day.

### ✅ What We Welcome

#### Kind & Supportive Messages

*   • Expressions of empathy and understanding
*   • Encouragement and motivation
*   • Gratitude and appreciation
*   • Genuine compliments
*   • Inspirational thoughts
*   • Well-wishes and blessings

#### Positive Communication

*   • Uplifting and hopeful language
*   • Warm and caring tone
*   • Respectful acknowledgment of struggles
*   • Universal messages of love and support
*   • Cultural wisdom and expressions
*   • Gentle reminders of worth and strength

### ❌ What We Don't Allow

#### Harmful Content

*   • Threats, harassment, or hateful language
*   • Personal attacks or discouraging remarks
*   • Graphic, sexual, or violent content
*   • Advice encouraging self-harm or illegal acts
*   • Excessive negativity that diminishes someone's day

#### Exploitative Requests

*   • Requests for money, donations, or gifts
*   • Phishing attempts or scams
*   • Requests for personal/financial information
*   • Sharing personal contact details
*   • Misleading claims or false promises

### ⚠️ Important Notes

**Benign Metaphors:** We understand that supportive messages may sometimes use challenging imagery ("weather the storm", "fight through difficulties") when the overall intent is encouraging.

**Acknowledging Struggle:** It's okay to acknowledge that someone may be going through tough times, as long as the overall message is uplifting and supportive.

**Names Are Allowed:** You can use first names in your messages, but please don't share specific contact information that could identify you.

### 🤖 AI Review Process

Every message is reviewed by our AI system to ensure it meets these guidelines. The AI looks for:

*   • Overall tone and intent of the message
*   • Presence of kind, supportive language
*   • Absence of harmful or exploitative content
*   • Cultural sensitivity and appropriateness

Thank you for helping us create a safer, kinder world, one message at a time.

## Privacy Policy

×

### Data Collection and Usage

At KindnessSender, we collect and use your personal information solely to optimize our website experience and facilitate the delivery of kind messages. We collect the following information:

*   **Email addresses:** Used to send and receive kind messages, prevent self-messaging, and manage unsubscribe requests
*   **Message content:** Processed for content validation, translation services, and delivery to recipients
*   **Location data (optional):** Country information shared voluntarily to enhance the recipient's experience
*   **Usage analytics:** Anonymous data to improve website functionality and user experience

### Our Commitment to Your Privacy

**We do NOT sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.** Your data is used exclusively to:

*   Deliver your kind messages to recipients
*   Prevent abuse and ensure message appropriateness
*   Improve our service quality and user experience
*   Comply with legal requirements when necessary

### Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. All data transmission is encrypted, and we regularly review our security practices.

### Your Rights

You have the right to unsubscribe from receiving messages at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email we send. You may also contact us to request information about or deletion of your personal data.
