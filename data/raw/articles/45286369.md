## "Stop — Think — Click"...

...is the basic recommendation for securely browsing the Internet and for securely reading emails. **Users who have followed this recommendation in the past were _less likely_ to have their computer infected or their computing account compromised**. However, _still too many users_ click on malicious web-links, and put their computer and account at risk.

Therefore, in order to avoid clicking at all, **all CERN users are asked to disconnect their computer mice from CERN computers**, and bring them to the CERN Animal Shelter for Computer Mice.

Let us help you:  
visit **[https://cern.ch/Computer.Security](https://cern.ch/Computer.Security)** or contact **[Computer.Security@cern.ch](mailto:Computer.Security@cern.ch)**
