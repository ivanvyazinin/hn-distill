# [![](/diffplug/gitcasso/raw/main/public/icons/icon-128.png)](/diffplug/gitcasso/blob/main/public/icons/icon-128.png) Gitcasso

[](#-gitcasso)

_Syntax highlighting and autosave for comments on GitHub (and other markdown-friendly websites)._

*   "Syntax highlighting is the lie that enables us to see the truth."
*   "The meaning of life is to find your lost comment drafts. The purpose of life is to post them."
