No fluff, no lock-in — just a clean API, clear docs, and tooling that plays nice with your existing stack. Run your first browser task in 4 lines of code.

```
from smooth import SmoothClientsmooth_client = SmoothClient(api_key=“cmzr-YOUR_API_KEY“)task = smooth_client.run(“Go to google flights and find the cheapest flight from London to Paris today“)print(f“Live URL: {task.live_url()}“)print(f“Agent response: {task.result()}“)
```
