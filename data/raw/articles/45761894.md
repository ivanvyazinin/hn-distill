# Recantha's Tiny Toolkit

By: [Mike Horne](/by/mike-horne)

* * *

A general purpose kit used for various purposes including a fair amount of stationery bits to help with design.

## Case

[![](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/TinyTookit_exterior.cca434c156f07c5a.jpg)](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/TinyTookit_exterior.dded8696de955046.jpg)

[Lihit Lab Large Maroon Camo Book-Type Pen Case](https://cultpens.com/products/lihit-lab-triple-pen-case?variant=43696392339699), LL00005-MC

## Contents

### Exterior

The exterior of the case has some pockets, so I've added some sticky notes.

[![](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/StickyNotes.64a89e3e9d89cb4c.jpg)](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/StickyNotes.793ba5f579a2c71a.jpg)

*   500 thin sticky notes or markers:

### Layer 1 - Multitool, scissors and penknife

My first layer is dominated by a fold-up multitool (see below), plus there's a pair of (rubbish) scissors and a Swiss Army knife.

[![](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/FirstLayer.c9ff6cba3c71dcc9.jpg)](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/FirstLayer.680beaca0957e02c.jpg)

*   [Multitool](https://www.temu.com/goods.html?_bg_fs=1&goods_id=601099815283715&sku_id=17593423836050) - this one was from Temu
*   Scissors
*   Swiss Army knife

[![](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/Multitool.d1c6054e7f7cd625.jpg)](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/Multitool.4bf1137cd2d4ef61.jpg)

### Layer 2.1 - Cutters, grips and pen

[![](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/SecondLayerLeft.776623139f5fe6ee.jpg)](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/SecondLayerLeft.0b97d7909d413f73.jpg)

*   Chisel-tip black Sharpie
*   Pair of side cutters
*   Scalpel (mind your fingers!)
*   Small Stanley adjustable spanner/wrench
*   Two box/craft cutters (of many!)

### Layer 2.2 - Miscellaneous

[![](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/SecondLayerRight.9e64d663253c4642.jpg)](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/SecondLayerRight.c0aaf59428d38ca7.jpg)

*   Small screwdriver handle
*   Set of screwdriver bits plus a socket adapter
*   Wire stripper/cutter
*   Tweezers of various configurations.

[![](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/SecondLayerRightImplements_1.0233fea7f659b226.jpg)](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/SecondLayerRightImplements_1.e2bfa22d73a0cc15.jpg)

*   Small adjustable spanner/wrench
*   Another box cutter.
*   Two pencils I picked up for free from eSpares
*   Small brush (suitable for clearing cr@p out from a keyboard)
*   Large pair of tweezers.

### Layer 3 - Cables and stationery

[![](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/ThirdLayer.989134c1a4e65163.jpg)](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/ThirdLayer.7bdeb605afc180ab.jpg)

*   Collection of USB cables and USB 2-way splitter
*   Paper clips of different sizes and colours!
*   Bulldog clip in there
*   microSD to full sized SD adapter.

[![](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/ThirdLayerLeftImplements.39f968e867324564.jpg)](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/ThirdLayerLeftImplements.dce7836b21260e92.jpg)

*   Another multitool
*   Small (circuit board) ruler
*   Small pair of tweezers
*   Another box cutter (!)
*   Socket set
*   An iFixit prise tool (the blue bit of plastic)
*   Small highlighter
*   Tiny measuring tape
*   Eraser.

[![](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/ThirdLayerRightImplements.69c4e87448067045.jpg)](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/ThirdLayerRightImplements.b65d65203fd621a0.jpg)

[![](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/HammerMultitool.38deb57929581d66.jpg)](https://tinytoolk.it/processed_images/HammerMultitool.398fb898568b4d18.jpg)

It's quite a heavy kit, because of the multitools, but it's very handy!

As you can see in my Tiny Tookit, there’s a bit of duplication (especially box cutters!), but not much space left. I intend to expand the kit when I can to include an electrical-size screwdriver, some electrical tape and other stuff I can find as we go along and then get rid of the duplicates to make space.
