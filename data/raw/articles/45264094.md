# Will I run Boston 2026?

A prediction for the 2026 Boston Marathon cutoff time, using our 2025 method.

By GetFast Team

[Last year](/blogs/boston-2025), we did a deep dive predicting the cutoff for the 2025 Boston Marathon. After the BAA announced the number of accepted time-qualified runners, our method was with 6 seconds of the actual cutoff.

This year, we're revisiting our model with an additional data point. With the recent announcement of **33,267 applicants** for the 2026 [race](https://www.baa.org/2026-boston-marathon-presented-bank-america-registration-update), our updated model predicts a buffer of **5 minutes and 16 seconds**.

This time is based on an assumption: the BAA will allow 24,000 time-qualified runners.[1](#footnote-1) But what if the number was different? We've built a small interactive tool for you to explore the possibilities.
