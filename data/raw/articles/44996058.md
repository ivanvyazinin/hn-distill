Your phone is a graveyard of withered digital trees, each one a monument to a promise you broke to yourself. You're a collector of good intentions you'll abandon by lunchtime. You've been coddled by an industry that profits from your failure by selling you toys instead of real tools.  
  
Enough.  
  
This is not a cute focus app.  
  
This is your personal defense system against a multi-trillion dollar industry designed to steal every second of your attention.  
  
The only way to use this app is to put the phone away. Break the pact, and we take your progress. No mercy.  
  
This is the last focus app you'll need because this is the hardest and most effective focus app on this planet.  
  
THE PROTOCOL IS NOT OPTIONAL:  
  
We don't have a start button because your life has already started, and you're wasting it. Put your phone away. That's the only way in.  
  
Pick it up, and we will scream siren, a siren so loud it will break you. It is the sound of your own weakness. All progress will be taken away if you don't put it back. We punish failure to build discipline.  
  
THIS IS NOT A FREE APP.  
  
THIS IS THE HARDEST FOCUS APP AND REQUIRES SUBSCRIPTION OR LIFETIME PURCHASE TO USE.  
  
  
You think this is expensive? The cost of your distraction is your entire future. The price of this app is a filter designed to keep out the tourists and the excuse-makers. If you can't afford it, you're not the person we're building this for.  
  
This will be the most hated and the most effective app on your phone.
