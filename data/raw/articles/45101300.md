## Know before you click. Stay in control as you browse.

Firefox helps you preview links and keep tabs tidy so nothing slows you down.

![Link previews illustration](/media/img/firefox/whatsnew/whatsnew142/link-previews.png)

Link Previews

### See what's behind the link before you click.

Link Previews show a snapshot of a page before you open it, helping you decide what’s worth your time. Just long press any link to preview and reduce distractions.

[Learn More](https://connect.mozilla.org/t5/discussions/try-link-previews-in-firefox-142-see-what-s-behind-a-link-before/m-p/103496#M40177)

![Tab groups illustration](/media/img/firefox/whatsnew/whatsnew142/tab-groups.png)

AI-Enhanced Tab Groups

### Your tabs, automatically grouped by topic.

A local AI model identifies similar tabs, automatically organizes them into groups, and even suggests group names, helping you stay organized. Everything happens on your device to respect your privacy.

[Learn More](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/how-use-ai-enhanced-tab-groups)

[See Full Release Notes](/firefox/142.0.1/releasenotes/)

## Tailor Firefox to fit your flow.

Stay organized, find what you need, and browse your way.

### [Less clutter and more focus with vertical tabs](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/use-sidebar-access-tools-and-vertical-tabs)

Firefox’s new sidebar lets you move tabs to the side, pin key sites and keep your AI assistant handy.

[Learn more](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/use-sidebar-access-tools-and-vertical-tabs)

Create a space that’s uniquely yours. Set the mood with Firefox wallpapers, custom colors, or your own photo. Open a new tab and click the icon to start customizing.

[Learn more](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/customize-items-on-firefox-new-tab-page#w_change-whats-on-your-new-tab-page)

### [Take control of your browsing with tab groups](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/tab-groups)

No more tab overload. Stay organized and in control of your time and attention. Drag one tab onto another to create your first group.

[Learn more](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/tab-groups)

## What’s New on Firefox Mobile?

New tools for focus, privacy, and smoother mobile browsing.

![](/media/img/firefox/whatsnew/whatsnew142/shield.svg)Privacy![](/media/img/firefox/whatsnew/whatsnew142/android-icon.png)Android

### Private tabs that stay private

Your private tabs lock automatically when you step away — and only unlock with your face, fingerprint, or PIN.

![](/media/img/firefox/whatsnew/whatsnew142/language-icon.svg)Language![](/media/img/firefox/whatsnew/whatsnew142/android-icon.png)Android

### Getting even more multilingual

Now translate web pages into Japanese, Chinese, Korean and more, so you can browse in your preferred language.

![](/media/img/firefox/whatsnew/whatsnew142/shield.svg)Security![](/media/img/firefox/whatsnew/whatsnew142/apple-icon.png)iOS

### Smarter passwords, fewer hassles

Firefox suggests strong passwords when you’re creating a new account on any site, and keeps them secure, ready on any device when you sync.

![](/media/img/firefox/whatsnew/whatsnew142/design-icon.svg)Design![](/media/img/firefox/whatsnew/whatsnew142/apple-icon.png)iOS

### A cleaner look with sharper focus

A streamlined UI and upgraded dark mode on Firefox for iOS bring clarity and calm to everything you browse.
